The grove grows deadlier and stranger. Corrupted village ruins now litter the woods, no two nights generate alike, the hunters come in four flavors, and the dark closes in around you.

- **New: a ruined Kweebec village.** Corrupted houses, shops, wells, guard towers, lampposts, and bridges (the grove's own tree-folk architecture, blighted) now scatter through the woods and stand at chosen anchors as cover, chokepoints, and landmarks. The village well centerpiece is corrupted to match.
- **New: every night is different.** Each round rolls a fresh world: the terrain, the trees, the caves, the shrine ring, and which ruins appear all change from run to run.
- **New: round mutators and more presets.** Stackable modifiers (Swarm, Relentless, Caverns, Creeping Decay, Blitz) bend a round, plus four new presets: Endless Night, The Swarm, Pitch Black, and Blitz.
- **New: hunter variety.** The Blighted Kweebec is no longer alone. A Lunger charges, a Spitter kites and keeps its distance, and an Ambusher blinks toward you. The roster grows and shifts as corruption climbs.
- **New: the grove hunts your senses.** A proximity dread vignette darkens and tightens as a hunter closes in, catches and near-misses hit with a jumpscare (camera shake, a scream, a hard flash), whispers and false cues rise with the corruption, and the music sinks deeper as the night turns.

This is an in-development build. The new worldgen, hunter behaviors, and scare effects are pending live playtest; the server build is green.
