The chase is playable. A full co-op round runs from "/kweebec start" to a win or loss, with the Blighted Kweebec on your heels.

- **New: the Chase ("Relight & Escape").** Drop into a Void-blighted grove at frozen midnight, relight the shrines while a corrupted Kweebec hunts you, then escape through the Heartwood Gate. Win if anyone gets out; lose if it cocoons you all or the night runs out.
- **New: co-op with downed-player presence.** Caught players are cocooned in roots, not removed. A teammate can free them. Solo plays the same loop alone.
- **New: difficulty presets.** Amateur, Nightmare, and Hardcore tune the rescues, the hunter, the shrine count, and how fast the corruption climbs. Pick one with "/kweebec start nightmare".
- **New: dread feedback.** A frozen dark sky, a tightening 3-tier heartbeat, title-card splashes, danger toasts, and a round HUD tracking the clock, shrines, who's still up, and the corruption meter.

This is an in-development build. A first live playtest surfaced and fixed the initial spawn-point, HUD-text, and game-mode issues; the round logic is complete, the server build is green, and broader playtesting continues.
