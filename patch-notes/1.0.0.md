The first public release. Gather your friends, relight the grove, and get out alive. The co-op
chase ("Relight & Escape") is here in full: a fresh Void-blighted grove every round, a hunter that
learns the night, glow-mushrooms to throw, a capstone boss on the harder nights, and a co-op escape
you have to earn together.

- **Relight and escape, 1 to 4 players (or solo).** Light the corrupted grove-shrines to open the
  Heartwood Gate, then gather on the extraction platform and hold it together until everyone gets out.
  Go down and you are cocooned in roots until a friend cuts you free.
- **A hunter that hunts you differently.** The Blighted Kweebec tracks the noise of your rituals and
  grows faster as the grove darkens, and different breeds stalk you in different ways.
- **The Warden wakes.** On the harder nights a multi-phase corrupted Warden rises when the gate opens.
  It is tougher the bigger your party and the higher the difficulty, you keep it killable by hurling
  glow-mushrooms at it, and it shows on your map so you always know where it is.
- **Glowing mushrooms to throw, and to mend.** Gather corrupted glowcaps and hurl them: stun the hunter,
  knock it flying, slow it to a slog, or blast for real damage. Scooping them up mends your wounds too,
  more on the gentler nights and the barest trickle on the harshest, so working the grove for mushrooms
  is half of staying alive.
- **Better runs, better loot.** Win and the spoils roll from a per-difficulty table that pays out more
  the better you played, with the rarest finds gated behind a strong run. The harder grove drops the
  richer loot.
- **Built for dread.** Perpetual midnight, thickening fog, a proximity heartbeat that tightens as the
  hunter nears, whispers in the trees, cinematic title cards, a triumphant sting when you escape, and a
  Void scream when the night takes you.
- **Better with MMO Skill Tree (optional), never required.** Installed alongside it, a round can advance
  your skills and rewards; on its own, it grants its own.

This is a fresh launch off a mod-jam build. If something feels off in your server, please report it on
the Versions tab so it can be fixed.
