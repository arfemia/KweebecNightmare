A small stock, packaging, and compatibility update on top of the first release: more Emberbloom for
the Warden fight, a leaner standalone install, and Artillery training on skill-tree servers.

- **More Emberbloom in the crypts.** The dungeon crypts and their chests spawn noticeably more
  Emberbloom, so the damage-bursting glowcap is easier to stock before the Warden wakes.
- **Leaner install: two companion mods required.** Kweebec Nightmare ships as a standalone jar and
  expects **Ziggfreed's CommonLib** and **Perfect Utils** installed alongside it (the previous
  release bundled them in). Drop all three jars into your `Mods/` folder; the server loads the two
  companions first.
- **Better with MMO Skill Tree: throw a bloom, train Artillery.** On a server running MMO Skill Tree,
  landing a thrown glow-mushroom on an enemy trains your **Artillery** skill. It needs a current MMO
  Skill Tree that reads the throw; without the skill tree, nothing changes and the minigame grants its
  own rewards as before.
