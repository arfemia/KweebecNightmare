The hunters got scarier. They watch you with glowing eyes from the dark, laugh when they
catch you out, and their blows hit far harder than before.

- **New: glowing-eyed hunters.** The Blighted Kweebec now wears a corrupted, darkened look and
  stares out of the night with glowing eyes, so you can feel one watching before you see the rest
  of it.
- **New: the hunters snicker.** When a hunter is close, or when it lands a hit on you, it has a
  chance to let out a low, eerie cackle from its own direction, a taunting "I see you" cue layered
  over the heartbeat and whispers.
- **New: far more devastating attacks.** A hunter's hit now strikes for much more than before and
  launches you back, and how hard each one hits depends on what kind it is: the charging Lunger
  hits hardest, the ranged Spitter the softest, with the Stalker and Ambusher in between.

Choosing your descent is its own screen now. Tell the Grove Warden a difficulty and you decide how
you face it: with anyone who answers the call, with your own coven, or alone.

- **New: the Enter the Nightmare screen.** Picking a difficulty no longer drops you straight into a
  queue. It opens a screen where you choose how to play: Public (brave the grove with whoever else
  is waiting), Coven (gather your own survivors first), or Alone (step into the dark by yourself,
  right now). Each is a card with its own icon.
- **New: the gathering counts down on screen.** Once you are waiting, the screen shows who is with
  you and a launch timer that ticks down live as the grove pulls you in, instead of a static notice.
- **New: covens carry your difficulty.** When you queue as a coven, it now enters the difficulty you
  chose, and the coven can stay an open gathering that fills with strangers or seal itself to your
  own members.
- **New: which ways you can enter are per-difficulty.** Each difficulty decides on its own which of
  Public, Coven, and Alone it offers, so a harder night can ask you to bring company.

This is an in-development build. The new hunter look, snicker cues, attack tuning, and the Enter
the Nightmare screen are pending live playtest; the server build is green.
