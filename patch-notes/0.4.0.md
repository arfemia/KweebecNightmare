The grove gives you something to fight back with. Glowing Moonbloom now grows among the
blight; gather it, spend it to cleanse the shrines, or throw it to stun the things that
hunt you. Every night you survive is now scored and ranked.

- **New: Moonbloom, the glowing mushroom.** It grows in clusters by the shrines and scattered
  through the grove, and the grove regrows a fresh batch partway through the night. Harvest it to
  carry charges.
- **New: cleanse the shrines with Moonbloom.** A shrine is no longer relit by standing and waiting;
  you spend Moonbloom at it and it lights at once. How much each shrine costs is configurable.
- **New: throw Moonbloom to stun the hunter.** A thrown Moonbloom bursts and freezes a Blighted
  Kweebec in place for a few seconds, buying you a window to run, revive a teammate, or finish a
  cleanse.
- **New: every run is scored.** Each player earns points for finishing fast, for taking little
  damage, and a small bonus for every hunter they stun. The weights are configurable.
- **New: a leaderboard.** Best score and best escape time are recorded per player and ranked by party
  size, kept across server restarts. Check yours with `/kweebec score` or the board with
  `/kweebec leaderboard`.

This is an in-development build. The new throw-to-stun, gather, and scoring are pending live
playtest; the server build is green.
