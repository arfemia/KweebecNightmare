The grove turns on you. Alongside the co-op chase, Kweebec Nightmare now offers player-versus-player:
take a friend, or a stranger, into the blight and fight it out as duelling saplings.

- **New: Kweebec Clash, a 1v1 and 2v2 brawl.** Step into a sealed arena as two teams and fight to the
  last sapling standing. With both teams still alive at the timer, the team that landed the most hits
  takes it; a tie goes to a short sudden-death overtime before a draw is called.
- **New: Kweebec Domination, a control-point fight.** Instead of trading blows to the death, hold the
  marked ground: stand on a point long enough to capture it, then bank points for as long as your team
  keeps it. First team to the score wins, or the team ahead when the clock runs out.
- **New: pick how a round is won.** A PvP preset chooses its rules: last team standing, most hits, most
  kills, or team deathmatch to a kill count, paired with single-life elimination, a limited number of
  lives, or endless respawns.
- **New: you fight as a Kweebec Sapling.** For a PvP match both teams are reskinned to the Kweebec
  Sapling, so the grove's own little folk are the ones squaring off.
- **New: grab a mushroom and use it as a weapon.** The arenas seed throwable mushrooms you can scoop up
  mid-fight: a knockback burst to shove a rival off a point or off a ledge, and a stun cloud to freeze
  them in place for the follow-up.
- **New: a host to send you in.** A new Grove warden NPC greets you with dialogue and lets you choose
  Clash or Domination, the team size, and the difficulty before the grove pulls you in.
- **New: arenas and presets are data.** The PvP modes, their rules, and the arenas they play out in are
  all authored as assets, so the offering can be retuned and expanded without a new build.
- **New: thrown mushrooms hit harder on the gentler difficulties.** A thrown Moonbloom and Emberbloom now
  deal a per-difficulty amount of damage, tuned so the easier grove rewards a good throw more: Moonbloom
  hits hard on Amateur and is a near-pure stun on Hardcore, while the boss-busting Emberbloom lands its
  biggest blows on Amateur and its smallest on Hardcore. Moonbloom still freezes what it hits, and a
  mushroom that splashes a teammate stays harmless.
- **New: escape with better spoils when you play better.** Win the chase and the reward chest now rolls
  from a per-difficulty loot table instead of a fixed handout: a guaranteed staple plus a pool of life
  essence and throwable mushrooms (Moonbloom, Gustbloom, Mirebloom, Emberbloom) whose haul grows with
  your run score, and the harder grove drops the richer loot. The rarest finds only show up after a
  strong run.

This is an in-development build. The Clash and Domination modes, the Sapling reskin, the mushroom
pickups, the new host NPC, and the asset-driven presets and arenas are pending live playtest; the
server build is green.
