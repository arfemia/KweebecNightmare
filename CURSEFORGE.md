# Kweebec Nightmare

**A standalone co-op horror chase. Relight the grove before the nightmare catches you.**

Night has fallen on the Emerald Grove, and the gentle Kweebec tree-folk have been twisted by the Void into something that hunts. You and up to three friends are saplings who must relight the corrupted grove-shrines to open the Heartwood Gate and escape, but every shrine you light draws the Blighted Kweebec closer, and lighting the last one wakes it for a frantic chase to the exit.

> Status: in development (mod-jam build). Early access build; the full round loop is still being built. Watch the Versions tab for playable releases.

## Features

- **Co-op horror, 1 to 4 players.** Work together to relight the grove, or get cocooned in roots and hope a friend cuts you free. Plays solo against an AI hunter too.
- **A hunter that learns the night.** The Blighted Kweebec tracks the noise of your rituals and grows faster and more relentless as the grove darkens.
- **Risk that scales with success.** Every shrine you light brings you closer to escape and closer to the hunt. The final shrine is the loudest of all.
- **A fresh night every time.** A new instanced grove per round keeps it rapidly repeatable.
- **Built for dread.** Perpetual midnight, thickening fog, a proximity heartbeat that tightens as the hunter nears, and cinematic title cards.
- **Better with MMO Skill Tree (optional).** Installed alongside MMO Skill Tree, surviving a night advances your skills and rewards; on its own, it grants its own rewards. Never required.

## Install

Place `KweebecNightmare-<version>.jar` in your server `Mods/` folder. Requires a Hytale server in the Update 5 range.
